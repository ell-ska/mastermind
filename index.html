Welcome to Mastermind

The objective of the game is to guess the exact positions of the colors in the computer chosen sequence. The sequence is made up by four colors, duplicates are allowed.

Fill your line with guesses by clicking the colors to the left.

You can go back one color

or remove all colors

from your active line. If you run out of ideas you can use

the randomizer.

You can change your guesses until you submit them.

Once submited the computer will display a response to the result of your guesses:

A black dot means both the correct color and the position is correct.

A gray dot means the color is correct but the positions is not.

You win the game if you manage to guess all the colors and their positions in the sequense.

You lose the game if you use all guesses and don't figure out the sequense. You have 12 guesses in total.

Good luck!
